Do higher drafted players get more minutes in games?

The top 3 picks seem to get a large amount of playing time right away (because their team is probably more desparate). At about pick 15 there is a huge decline in minutes played, as these players may never be starters in their career. So this can account for the results of field goal % and points per game disparities. Players drafted higher are usually needed by their respective teams to step in right away and play. So they get more minutes and therefore a higher chance of scoring more points.
